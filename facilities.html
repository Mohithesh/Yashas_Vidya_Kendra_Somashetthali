Facilities

LAB

The labs are furnished with all necessary instruments and accessories to ensure that the practical understanding of the subject is comprehensive and complete.

SMART CLASS ROOM

Smart classes use all interactive modules like videos and presentations and these visually attractive methods of teaching become appealing to students of all categories.

PHYSICAL EDUCATION

We make sure children receive all the physical exercise they need for the growth, fitness, self discipline of the body and mind that is ready for everything. Yoga and meditation are also given equal importance.

TRANSPORT

The school runs its own transport fully in compliance with the latest RTO regulations laid by the Govt. of Karnataka. The drivers are experienced and each van has a dedicated helper who helps the students to board the van.
